Qafqaz Tufandag Mountain Resort Hotel

3600, Gabala AZ, Gebele

Located in Gabala, 300 m from L6 Qafqaz Gondola Lift, Qafqaz Tufandag Mountain Resort Hotel has ski-to-door access and a fitness centre, as well as free WiFi. Featuring a 24-hour front desk, this property also welcomes guests with a restaurant and a terrace. L5 Duruca Gondola Lift is 400 m from the hotel.

At the hotel, the rooms are equipped with a wardrobe and a flat-screen TV. Rooms are complete with a private bathroom fitted with a hair dryer, while some rooms at Qafqaz Tufandag also have a seating area.

A continental or American breakfast is available each morning at the property. Guests relax in the well-equipped wellness area, which features a sauna. The area is popular for skiing, and ski equipment hire is available at the accommodation. For guests' convenience, Qafqaz Tufandag Mountain Resort Hotel has a business centre. L4 Gabala Gondola Lift is 1.9 km from the hotel, while L2 Riverside Gondola Lift is 3.4 km away. This property also has one of the best-rated locations in Gabala! Guests are happier about it compared to other properties in the area.
