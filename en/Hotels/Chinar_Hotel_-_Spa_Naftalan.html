Chinar Hotel & Spa Naftalan

Shirvan Prospekti 12, AZ4600 Naftalan, Azerbaijan

Get the celebrity treatment with world-class service at Chinar Hotel & Spa Naftalan.

 Chinar Hotel & Spa is located in the Naftalan Resort, and is surrounded with a beautiful pine and platan forest. It offers a spa centre and several restaurants. Free Wi-Fi and free private parking are provided. All spacious, air-conditioned rooms come with a flat-screen TV, a minibar, a safety deposit box and a kettle. Slippers, bathrobes and a hairdryer are available in a bathroom. Saphire Restaurant serves various Azerbaijan and European dishes. It is possible to have a drink at Firuza Lobby Bar or to order a fresh fruit cocktail at Vitamin Bar. There is also Paradiso Night Club on site. Chinar offers different medical and cosmetic treatment including naftalan procedures, hydrotherapy and massage services. Guests can work out in the gym, swim in the indoor swimming pool and relax in the sauna or Turkish steam bath. Natalan city centre is 5 minutes’ drive away from the hotel, and Ganja International Airport is 50 km away.
