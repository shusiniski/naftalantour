Garabag Resort&Spa

Shirvan Prospekti 12, AZ4600 Naftalan, Azerbaijan

Experience world-class service at Garabag Resort&Spa Garabag Resort&Spa offers health improvement procedures featured at Entera Spa and Wellness Centre. It provides a Turkish steam bath, a sauna, a cedar barrel and a salt room. The procedures are performed using a mixture of naftalan oil, salts and seaweed of the Caspian Sea and volcanic mud. Cosmetic and medical treatments and a hair salon are also available. Each room at this resort is air conditioned and features a TV with satellite channels. A terrace or a balcony can be found in certain rooms. For your comfort, you will find bathrobes, slippers and free toiletries. Complimentary water and coffee/tea facilities are avialbale for free in all rooms. Guests can order a meal in the on-site restaurant. A buffet restaurant with dietary options is also available. The property has a fitness centre where you can do pilates, yoga and aerobics. Guests can play tennis, billiards, mini golf, basketball or volleyball, or enjoy a swim in the outdoor pool. There is also a children's playground and various entertainment programmes are offered. The property offers different medical and cosmetic treatment. Ganja International Airport is 50 km from Garabag Resort&Spa. This property also has one of the best-rated locations in Naftalan! Guests are happier about it compared to other properties in the area.
