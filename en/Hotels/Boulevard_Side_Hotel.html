Boulevard Side Hotel

3, Ayaz Ismayilov St, Bakı AZ1025

Boulevard Side Hotel is located on the shores of the Caspian Sea in Baku, next to the Seaside Boulevard. Free WiFi access is available. Each room here will provide you with a satellite TV, air conditioning and a minibar. Featuring a shower, each private bathroom also comes with a hairdryer and bathrobes. At Boulevard Side Hotel you will find a 24-hour front desk, a bar and a snack bar. Other facilities offered at the property include meeting facilities, dry cleaning and an ironing service. The property offers free parking. The hotel is 3.3 km from Qiz Qalasi, 17.7 km from Heydar Aliyev International Airport and 2.8 km from Tofik Bakhramov Stadium.
