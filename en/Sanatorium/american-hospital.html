ABOUT AMERICAN HOSPITAL

American Hospital was founded in 1920 under the leadership of Amiral Mark L. Bristol. In his hospital opening speech he stated ‘All races, religions and nations need to be given equal health services; Turkey needs to overcome this by training nursers to resolve this issue urgently by establishing a School of Nursing; Americans, Turks and all other nations should be provided with the American standard health care services.’

The American Hospital was founded on August 20th 1920 and the first place it was established was 67 Çarşıkapı Street at Eski Istanbul a triplex wooden mansion. It served the public with 45 beds and 3 operating theaters.

Having used a temporary building in the 1925’s from the German Hospital located in Taksim, American Hospital moved to an apartment in Tesvikiye Street and continued to serve the public in the 1928’s.

American Hospital located in Nisantasi is where we are currently located, on November 14th1939 for the first time the hospital began serving at a specially new designed building.

In 1972 a seven floored Acheson building, in 1988 a addition ten floored outpatients building, in 1991 a new emergency service, in 1994 a extended entrance with 2600 m2 to 16.000m2 added.

Vehbi Koç Foundation joined in year 1995. Since that day the hospital was a New York based hospital. Managed and supported by an non- profit American Hospital Corporation.

Since 1995 the Vehbi Koc Foundation contributed over $200 million to offer modern large scale renovations and new buildings, in year 2007 a 60.500 m2 evolved into a modern health facility.

92 years ago the hospital was established with 45 beds, 3 operating rooms where as today has 200 patient rooms, 62 intensive care beds and 12 operating beds, approximately 120 outpatients, 20 thousand in patients get treated and over 10 thousand leaders which carry out surgeries have enable to become a leading and reliable health care provider.

American Hospital is a private health institution within the Vehbi Koç Foundation.

Physicians, nurses and patient care quality, its diagnosis and treatment technologies, the practiced management systems and processes implemented by North American standards for 92 years the Vehbi Koç Foundation American Hospital has played a key role in the development of the health sector in our country.

The American Hospital has gained respect in the eyes of the physicians, nurses, healthcare professionals and patients with its ethical approach and sense of trust.
